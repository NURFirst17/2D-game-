RULES
1. All game-specific code, scenes, prefabs, configs and content go only into Assets/NURbay.
2. Imported packs must stay in Assets/ThirdParty and should not be mixed into NURbay directly.
3. Animations folder stores .anim and .controller assets. Source PNG sprite sheets belong in Art or Sprites.
4. Temporary recovery files stay in Assets/_Recovery and should not be referenced by gameplay scenes.
